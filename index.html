Aurenor

Follow the Light.
Restore the Lost.

Aurenor's basically a puzzle game where you're putting together these crystal fragments to uncover stories told through ancient symbols.

How It Works

  • Find a Shard

    Every chamber holds a shattered crystal waiting to be understood — touch it to begin reading its pattern.

  • Align the Glyph

    You rotate and layer the crystal pieces until they click into place - there's this really satisfying hum when you get it right.

  • Watch the World Return

    Every puzzle you solve actually rebuilds part of the world map, opening up new areas and revealing more of the story.

Features

  • Resonance Chains

    When you solve a puzzle, it doesn't just unlock the next area - it actually changes future puzzles, so your choices really matter.

    Tactile Crystal Mechanics

    The crystal pieces have this nice weight to them when you rotate them - we tried to make it feel like you're actually handling something physical.

  • A World Written in Light

    No long cutscenes here - the story unfolds as you solve puzzles, so if you pay attention, you'll pick up on the details.

    No Timers. No Penalties.

    Take your time with Aurenor - it's all about thinking through the puzzles, not racing against the clock.

About

Aurenor was made for players who actually stop to look around and soak in the atmosphere. We think puzzles should feel like you're discovering something cool, not doing homework. Each room is designed to surprise you, but when you solve it, it should feel obvious in hindsight. We're a small independent studio obsessed with light, texture, and that awesome moment when everything suddenly makes sense.
